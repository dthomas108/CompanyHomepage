Mission Statement

XYZ is dedicated to improving lives through fincancial education, and exposure to underrepresented career fields amongst minority populations. Our goal is to provide resources which will afford under-funded communities access to training and education in fields were minorities typically face financial barriers to entry. Reducing and eliminating these road blocks to career paths where minorities represent less than 5% of the total demographic will increase generational exposure to more lucrative careers, increasing the median household income, allowing participants to become self-sufficient and insuring that funds, traditionally dedicated to low-income households, are redistributed through out the community for other purposes.

About Us

XYZ is a black-owned, woman-owned, non-profit focused on enriching the lives of people who come from similar under-priviliged backgrounds. Our team of "DreamMakers" consists of people from many backgrounds united by the XYZ Mission Statement. From Doctors to Lawyers, Entrepreneurs, Engineers and Tech-sperts of all levels and specialties, XYZ brings like-minded and commmunity-oriented individuals together to target problems and create solutions for people just like us. XYZ strives to bring personal experience and relatability to the communities and individuals we serve. This creates a sense of trust amongst our clients, knowing that people who have been in their shoes are present mentally and emotionally to support their goals and helping them tackle familiar obstacles along the way.
